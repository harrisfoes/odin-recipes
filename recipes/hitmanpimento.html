Hitman Pimento

Description:

This pimento cheese sandwich is the favorite amongst skilled freelancers who do all kinds of cleaning jobs--from search histories, to crime scenes!

This sandwich also happens to be the favorite amongst hitmen and bodyguards, but don't tell them I told you that!

Ingredients:

  • A piece of white bread
  • Pimento cheese spread
  • Annother piece of white bread

Steps:

  1. Take two sliced white bread
  2. Spread the correct amount of cheese into one of them
  3. Close it with the other bread

That's it! Pretty simple right?
